Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify the sender before making payments
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly check bank statements
- Use trusted and secure apps only
- Be careful while scanning QR codes

UPI Safety Tips:
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN
- Always double-check before approving any payment request

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
